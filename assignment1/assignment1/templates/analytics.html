Analysis and Findings (1), (2) and (3)

  1. The pattern I found for most users was mostly 1 drilldown in clicks once the answer to a question was found. This tells me that the user is more likely to make a google search and then visit stackoverflow for the problem's answer rather than use the website's search feature
